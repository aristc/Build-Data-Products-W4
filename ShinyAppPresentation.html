Application Overview

Data Source

The source is an open data website by SNCF, France's national state-owned railway company.

The chosen dataset is called "Regularite mensuelle Transilien" which displays the % of trains that arrived on time on a monthly basis.

URL: https://ressources.data.sncf.com/explore/dataset/ponctualite-mensuelle-transilien/

Interactivity

The chosen widgen input was "SelectInput".

The second plot displays the trend of train delays over time for one line only.

The user may chose the train line and visualize the monthly average delays (% of trips) for the selected line.
